# Blinkit_Dashboard

BUSINESS REQUIREMENT
  To conduct a comprehensive analysis of Blinkit's sales performance, customer satisfaction, and inventory distribution to identify key insights and oppurtinities using various KPIs and visualizations in Power BI.
  
KPI RQUIREMENTS:
 * Total Sales: The overall revenue generated from all items sold.
 * Average Sales: The average revenue per sale.
 * Number of Items: The total count of different items sold.
 * Average Rating: The average customer rating for item sold.
